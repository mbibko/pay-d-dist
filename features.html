    Our Features in detail

    Our features at a glance

    Payment Processing

    Payment Orchestration: Management and processing of payments including initiation and settlement of payment of products and services in your webpage and/or webapp.

    • Payment via API calls
    • Payment capturing, processing, execution and response
    • Set-up multiple merchants to receive funds
    • Refund payments via API or Merchant Panel

    Back‑End Systems Connection

    Integrated online payment solution for your business.

    • Connect Seamlessly: link your business to our payment system using our APIs for smooth data transfer between your Back-End and our gateway
    • Easy integration: Add our customizable payment page to your site or app (as an in-frame page or pop-up) to let customers enter their payment details and complete their purchase.

    Customer Wallet

    Offer a digital wallet for fast, secure transactions, with stored payment methods for easy checkouts

    • Offer a standard wallet function for secure payment storage within a single systems that integrates with your product
    • Offer an advanced ToyotaCustomer wallet function for secure payment storage
    • Offer one single Toyota PaymentID and Toyota CustomerWallet that can be used for purchases across multiple Toyota Webshops.

    Subscription Model

    Subscriptions offer regular, automatic payments for continuous access, with features like auto-renewal, easy cancellation, and secure processing.

    • Subscription setup: Create and manage subscription plans, including pricing, billing intervals, rules for charges, and cancellation options.
    • Definition of auto renewal policies

    Billing & Invoicing

    Invoicing and billing are integral components of the financial transaction process helping to maintain transparency, facilitate timely payments, and establish a clear record of business transactions between parties.

    • Creation and maintenance of billing schedules
    • Invoice generation for products or services rendered
    • Management of customer billing & payment preferences (e.g. Credit Card, Paypal, etc.)
    • Customer communication (via email) of callout for billing and payment related events

    Tax Calculation

    We provice the support and essential tools required to calculate and manage taxes according to the taxability rules specific to your products or services.

    • Support taxes for multi region & multiple VAT handling: management and processing of different VAT rates simultaneously; depending on the location and/or region requirements.
    • Management of tax rates by jurisdiction and application of taxes to billing transactions based on your product taxability rules.

    Analytics & Reporting

    Get regular operational and financial reports to track your transactions and monitor your business performance

    • Back office management: access to Merchant Panel (transactional information e.g. monthly payments, utilized payment methods, etc.)
    • Operational reporting: standardized reporting and data exports on a periodic or ad-hoc schedule
    • Financial reporting: reports on transactional data, revenue recognition and payment to facilitate finance and accounting
    • Enables the organization to analyse and leverage purchasing behaviour across all connected E-commerce systems

    Secure payments

    Every payment is going through our safe environment with multiple safety instances.

    • Tokenised transactions, PCI DSS Level 1, SSL /TLS certification, 3DS2 authentication, GDPR compliant
