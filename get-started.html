    Merchant

    Onboarding

    These are the general steps/documents to be provided

    1

    The merchants receive a link to start a digital onboarding process where all the information and documents are entered/submitted in a web form.

    2
    Merchants provide general information on the company

    1. 1. Legal name, VAT, type of business, address, etc.
    2. 2. Details on the ultimate beneficiary “UBO” (Usually CEO/Managing director)
    3. 3. Identification of the UBO(s)
    3
    Payout information

    Merchant will need to specify their pay-out information (e.g. Bank account data, pay-out frequency and a relevant contact person(s)

    4
    Supporting documents

    Merchants need to submit supporting documents, such as authorized signatory lists and identification, for the Know Your Customer (KYC) process during account creation. Document requirements vary based on merchant type and are communicated during onboarding.
